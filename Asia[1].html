Asian Foods

Here are many foods that are popular in Asia:
  1. Momos
  2. Kimchi
  3. Dim Sum
  4. Bulgogi BBQ
  5. Bun Cha
Momos
Momos are very popular in the Himalayas. Momos resemble dumplings and are usually filled with Himalayan meat or vegetable Momos are ususally served with hot chilli sauce, dark soy and a side of soup.

Kimchi
Kimchi is as popular as condiments are in America. In Korea, fermented vegetables or "Kimchi" is paired with most meals. The meal variations are endless.

Dim Sum
Dim Sum are dumplings that come in a variety of flavours.Traditionally, Dim Sum is served in bamboo steaming baskets.

Bulgogi BBQ
Korean Barbecue is a hands-on eating experience. Bulgogi is richly marinated beef. Bulgogi is one of the most popular Korean BBQ meat choices.

Bun Cha
Bun Cha is grilled pork served with rice noodles and a slightly hot dipping sauce. Compared to most veggie filled delicacies in Vietnam, Bun Cha is a bit on the "unhealthy" side. Bun Cha is usually a "lunch time" food or a mid-day snack."
